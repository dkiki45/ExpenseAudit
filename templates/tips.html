üß† Policy Designer Mindset

Best Practices
  • Use strict categories (Meal, Travel, Software).
  • Define explicit numeric thresholds.
  • Aim for binary outcomes (Approve/Reject).
  • Think like an auditor, not a chatbot.
What to Avoid
  • Vague terms like "reasonable" or "cheap".
  • Multiple exceptions in one sentence.
  • Subjective rules requiring context.
  • Contradictory logic.
